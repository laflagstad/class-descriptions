### Floristics

**Floristics Description:** This [level] is dominated by *Betula nana*, with at least 25% cover often in combination with low willow shrubs. The remaining cover is occupied by low or prostrate shrubs, like *Rhododendron tomentosum, Vaccinium uliginosum*, and *Salix pulchra*. Also common are the dwarf-shrubs *Vaccinium vitis-idaea* and *Kalmia procumbens*, and fruticose lichens belonging to the *Cladonia* genus.
