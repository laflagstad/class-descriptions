### Floristics

**Floristics Description:** This [level] represents plants highly tolerant of salt and saturation. Pioneer species like *Puccinellia phryganodes* and *Carex subspathacea* can be found at the seaward edge of barren mudflats. These are subsequently colonized by halophytic, succulent forbs such as *Stellaria humifusa* and *Cochlearia groenlandica*. Species characteristic of the high salt marsh are the sedges *Carex ramenskii* and *Carex glareosa*, the grasses *Dupontia fisheri* and *Calamagrostis deschampsoides*, and the forbs *Chrysanthemum arcticum* and *Hippuris tetraphylla*.
